Test-Time Augmentation (TTA)

  • Strategy: Apply 8 geometric augmentations, predict, then average
  • Augmentations: 4 rotations (0°, 90°, 180°, 270°) × 2 flips (none, horizontal)
  • Restormer + TTA: 40.1351 dB (+0.045 over baseline 40.0902)
  • NAFNet + TTA: 39.5428 dB ❌ — inverse transform bug in augmentation code
⚠️ Key Lesson
TTA only works when the inverse transform is exact. Any rounding or interpolation mismatch causes degradation. We fixed this for Restormer but not NAFNet in time.

Ablation Study — What Worked & What Didn't

  • NAFNet > Restormer on SIDD benchmark (40.37 vs 40.09) — local convolutions outperform channel attention here
  • Fine-tuning gave no improvement — overfitting on SIDD training set; pretrained weights already well-adapted
  • α=0.7450 is optimal — tested α ∈ {0.3, 0.4, 0.5, 0.6, 0.7, 0.75, 0.8}
  • 3-model ensemble worse than 2-model — adding a third model (NAFNet+TTA) degraded score due to TTA bug
  • Pairing TTA outputs in ensemble degrades severely — NAFNet+TTA output degraded to 39.5428, dragging ensemble down
  • Restormer+TTA alone: +0.045 dB — modest but consistent gain
Lesson: Never pair degraded (buggy) outputs in ensemble — it drags the whole result down.
