## 2. Models Implemented

### 2.1 NAFNet (Baseline)
- **Architecture**: Nonlinear Activation Free Network (CVPR 2022)
- **Pretrained Weight**: `NAFNet-SIDD-width64.pth` (464MB, from official repo)
- **Configuration**: width=64, middle_blks=12, enc_blks=[2,2,4,4], dec_blks=[4,4,2,2]
- **Expected PSNR**: ~39.98 dB on SIDD benchmark (from paper/leaderboard)

### 2.2 NAFNet + TTA (Test-Time Augmentation)
- **Method**: 8-way TTA — 4 rotations (0°, 90°, 180°, 270°) × 2 flips (none, horizontal)
- **Expected PSNR**: ~40.10–40.30 dB (+0.1–0.3 dB over baseline)
- **Note**: TTA increases inference time 8× but consistently improves PSNR

### 2.3 Restormer
- **Architecture**: Restormer (CVPR 2022) — transformer-based denoising network
- **Pretrained Weight**: Restormer_SIDD.pth (from official repo)
- **Expected PSNR**: ~40.06 dB on SIDD benchmark (from paper)
- **TTA Attempt**: OOM crashed on RTX 3080 Ti 12GB — insufficient VRAM for 8-way TTA

### 2.4 Ensemble v1 (Weighted Average)
- **Method**: Weighted average of NAFNet + TTA and Restormer outputs
- **Formula**: `alpha * NAFNet_TTA + (1-alpha) * Restormer`
- **Alpha grid tested**: 0.3, 0.4, 0.5, 0.6, 0.7
- **Note**: Ensemble CSVs generated; no Kaggle submission possible to validate best alpha
